Queen's Campus: Dead of Night
A first-person survival shooter set on the Queen's University campus at night. Fight your way through 8 iconic buildings, scavenge weapons and supplies, and escape before the clock runs out.
How to Play

WASD — Move
Mouse — Look
Left Click — Shoot
E — Pick up items
P — Pause

Objective
Find the grad student survivor in Grant Hall, collect the boat key, and reach the ferry dock — all within 10 minutes.
Enemies

Zombie Professors — slow but tough, takes 3 hits
Feral Frosh — fast and aggressive, takes 1 hit
Varsity Athletes — heavily built, takes 6 hits

Weapons

Pistol — starting weapon, 20 rounds
Shotgun — found in the ARC
Fire Axe — found in the Queen's Centre

Play in Browser
Open index.html in any modern browser — no installation required.
